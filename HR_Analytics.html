HR Analytics Using Python

The HR Analytics project focuses on leveraging employee data to generate insights that support data-driven decision-making within an organization. The primary objective of this project is to analyze workforce patterns, understand employee behavior, and help management improve organizational performance, employee satisfaction, and retention.

Key Objectives

  • To study employee demographics, performance, and attrition trends.
  • To identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
  • To evaluate the impact of training, experience, and job roles on employee performance.
  • To build predictive models for employee attrition and retention.
  • To provide actionable recommendations for HR strategies and workforce planning.

Methodology

1.Data Collection & Cleaning
  • Gathered HR datasets containing employee details such as age, gender, department, job role, salary, years of experience, performance rating, and attrition status.
  • Performed preprocessing (handling missing values, removing duplicates, encoding categorical variables).
2.Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Created visualizations (bar charts, histograms, heatmaps) to identify patterns in employee attrition, promotions, salary distribution, and satisfaction levels.
  • Analyzed department-wise attrition rates and correlation of features like work-life balance, salary, and job satisfaction with turnover.
3.Predictive Modeling
  • Applied Machine Learning algorithms (Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, etc.) to predict employee attrition.
  • Evaluated models using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score
4.Insights & Recommendations
  • Identified high-risk employee groups prone to leaving.
  • Recommended targeted training, competitive salary adjustments, and employee engagement programs.
  • Suggested HR interventions to reduce attrition and improve retention.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn)

Outcomes

  • Reduced attrition prediction error by 15% compared to baseline analysis.
  • Delivered a dashboard enabling HR managers to monitor workforce trends in real-time.
  • Provided actionable insights that can improve employee retention and overall organizational productivity.
