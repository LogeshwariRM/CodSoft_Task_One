Student Admission Form

The Student Admission Form project is designed to create a structured system for collecting, validating, and storing student information efficiently. The main objective is to simplify the admission and record-keeping process for educational institutions by providing a digital form with validation and backend connectivity.

Key Objectives

  • To collect essential student details such as name, age, gender, date of birth, email, phone number, address, and course preference.
  • To validate inputs (e.g., correct email format, mandatory fields, restricted age range).
  • To ensure user-friendly data entry with error messages for incorrect inputs.
  • To store collected student data securely in a backend database for future retrieval and analysis.
