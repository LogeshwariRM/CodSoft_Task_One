Travel Blog Website

I designed and developed a travel blog website using HTML and CSS, focusing on simplicity, readability, and calming aesthetics. The website showcases a collection of travel experiences presented as individual blog pages, each featuring a unique background color theme, relevant images, and engaging storytelling. The design emphasizes a clean layout with easy navigation, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the journey of each destination without distractions.

The blog includes six carefully crafted pages: A Journey to the Mountains, Exploring Coastal Beauty, The Magic of Ancient City, Discovering Hidden Waterfall, City Lights at Night, and A Taste of Local Culture. Each page contains a high-quality featured image and descriptive paragraphs that bring the place to life, highlighting its atmosphere, culture, and unique experiences. A soothing palette and consistent typography were chosen to create a sense of calm and connection throughout the website.

This project reflects my ability to build structured, multi-page websites with attention to user experience, storytelling through design, and responsive layouts. It demonstrates not only my technical skills in web development but also my creativity in blending visuals and text to create an engaging digital travel diary. By combining design elements with content, the project delivers an inviting and memorable browsing experience for users.
